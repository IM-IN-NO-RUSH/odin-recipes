Fried Chicken Batter

Descrption

This is a big batch of Fried Chicken Seaoning that is readily available, to save you time in the kitchen

Ingredients

  • 4 cups Flour
  • 1 cup Corn Starch
  • 4 tbsp Lawrys
  • 4 tbsp Creole Seasoning
  • 3 tbsp Garlic Powder
  • 3 tbsp Oniion Powder
  • 1 1/2 tbsp Salt
  • 3 tbsp Pepper
  • 1 Gallon Zip-Lock Bage

Steps

  1. Place all ingredients in zip-lock bag.
  2. Taste seasoning as you go and adjust to your liking
